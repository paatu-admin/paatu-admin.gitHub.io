Request Account Deletion – பாட்டு App

If you would like to delete your account and all associated data from the பாட்டு mobile app, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Send an email to: support@paatu.app
  2. Use the subject line: Delete My Paatu Account
  3. In your email, include:
    • The Google account email address you used to sign in
    • Any additional context if needed

We will delete your account and all related data within 5 business days of receiving your request.

If you have any questions, feel free to reach out.
